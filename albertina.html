Our Story

Welcome to Albertina's Kitchen, a personally owned restaurant in the heart of Ibadan, Nigeria. created in 2019, we've been serving authentic Nigerian dishes made with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients and traditional recipes .

My name is Tomi Albertina,owner of albertina kitchen, I started this business with a simple mission: to share the delicious flavors of Nigerian home cooking with her community. With a vision that started in the kitchen of my family home has now manifested to a fully owned business.

At Albertina's Kitchen, every dish is prepared with the same care and attention you'd find in a Nigerian home. We take pride in preserving our culinary heritage while creating a warm, welcoming space for everyone to enjoy.
